Our Mission

Provide individual personal coaching to young passionate athletes with the love of soccer. Our intimate training session will allow us to effectively identify areas of improvement for our trainee's, so they can gain a competitive edge in the modern game.

Services

*Age Groups: Under 8-17
*Maximum Number of Players Per Session: 10
*Minimum Number of Players for Small Group Training: 3
*Targeted Areas: Agility, Technique, Power, and Strength
*Location: Wismer Park (Spring/Summer/Fall), Various GTA Indoor Gyms (Winter)

1 on 1 Training

- Player gets the specific attention needed.
- Coach is a mentor & easily spot areas to improve.
- Individualized training program so players learn skills & technique faster.

Small Group Training

- For players who work best in small groups.
- Friendly competition to increase player enjoyment & motivation.
- Individual attention by ensuring a 3:1, 4:1, or 5:1 ratio of players to coaches.

Team Training

- For teams struggling to complete full training drills during practice.
- Perfect for strength training, conditioning, and fitness goals during the preseason.
- Teams train with coaches for extra reps.

Body Movement Assessment

- Identifies muscle weakness & imbalances
- Assessing level of player strength, on/off the field.
- Focus on flexibility & mobility throughout the hips, knees and shoulders
to help minimize the chances of an injury occurring.

Position Specific Training

- Improve understanding of their position, passing, spatial awareness and shooting.
- Works on how to position themselves on the field, effective turning, excellent receiving,and ensures proper body positioning

Speed and Agility

- Overcomes issue of frequently tripping over the ball or own feet
- Improves jumping, landing, ladder drills, and other soccer specific movements
- Emphasis on footwork.

Sports Specific Fitness

- Teaches dietary recommendations to help players receive the best nutrition for their sport
- Specialized training offering individualized training for individuals from sports other than soccer on occasion.

Online Training

- Remote training for clients not in GTA area
- Training programs perfectly suited for both athletes and non-athletes
- Ideal for maintaining social distancing guidelines during the COVID-19 pandemic while still receiving training

About Us

Tinashe Kusema, has been involved in the world of soccer for as long as he can remember. Although he is now a certified personal trainer, he has been a player for far longer. 
 
Starting back at Marietta High School in Marietta, Georgia, USA, he was molded into a star Forward/Midfielder and became the official MVP of a team that went undefeated at home in 5AAAAA Region.  
 
During his high school and collegiate career, Kusema played for a variety of clubs, including Marietta F.C. receiving the titles of Best Offensive Player, Team Co-Captain, Leading Goal Scorer, and Saturn Futbol Club, which they became League Champions and US National Open Cup Indoor Champions, it became clear that Kusema had a bright future in the world of soccer.

In 2007 Kusema migrated to Canada from the USA. Between 2008-16, he was a part of 3 different clubs. The first, Toronto Croatia Football Club. He also played with Brampton City United for 2 seasons, where his team became CSL Reserve Champions. There were then four seasons spent with Lakeshore United, which saw them winning the Ukrainian Tournament as well as become Champions of the in-season tournament. 
 
Since leaving Lakeshore United in 2016, Kusema has dedicated his time to teaching other younger players to go from good to great without losing a love of the sport. This is what led to him establishing Total Fitness Kinetics: Athletic Performance. 
 
With the goal of offering high-quality training programs for youth soccer players at an affordable price, TFK Athletic Performance is responsible for training dozens of future stars. And behind all of it is Kusema as he strives to teach and inspire players under 8-17.
